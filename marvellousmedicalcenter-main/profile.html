Dr. Roshini V. Nair

MDS, Prosthodontist

Email Dr. Roshini

Dr. Roshini V. Nair is a highly skilled Prosthodontist with over a decade of professional experience in the dental field. She embarked on her academic journey by earning her Bachelor’s degree in Dental Surgery from Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR University in 2012. Driven to deepen her expertise, she pursued a post-graduate degree, earning her Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences in Karnataka in 2018. Throughout her career, Dr. Roshini has had the privilege of working in esteemed dental clinic groups, including Vasan Dental Care in Trivandrum, where she honed her clinical skills and developed a patient-centered approach to treatment. Since moving to the UAE in 2019, she has continued to deliver exceptional dental care to a diverse patient population. Dr. Roshini's areas of expertise encompass a wide range of dental procedures, including the design and fabrication of dental crowns and bridges, smile designing, full mouth rehabilitation, removable dentures, and root canal treatments. She is particularly adept at performing delicate pediatric root canal procedures and designing space maintainers that are essential for children’s dental health. In addition to her clinical skills, Dr. Roshini is a multilingual practitioner, fluent in English, Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil, and Kannada, which allows her to communicate effectively with patients from various backgrounds. Her commitment to ongoing education and patient well-being makes her a trusted choice for those seeking comprehensive dental care.

Expertise:
  • Dental crowns and bridges
  • Smile designing
  • Root canal treatments

Dr. Anu Joseph

MDS, Orthodontist

Email Dr. Anu

Dr. Anu Joseph is an accomplished orthodontist committed to helping patients achieve their ideal smiles through personalized care tailored to individual needs. She earned her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics from Dr. MGR University in Chennai, India. With over five years of extensive experience across India and the UAE, she has cultivated a comprehensive skill set in orthodontic treatment and general dentistry. Her clinical expertise encompasses a variety of advanced orthodontic techniques, including functional appliance therapy, which focuses on correcting jaw and dental alignment through customized devices. Dr. Anu Joseph is adept at providing both removable and fixed orthodontic solutions, as well as TAD-assisted (Temporary Anchorage Device) orthodontic care, which allows for greater precision in complex cases. She also offers clear aligners, an increasingly popular alternative to traditional braces, enabling patients to achieve straight teeth with minimal impact on their daily lives. In addition to her proficiency in orthodontics, Dr. Anu Joseph is skilled in several other dental procedures. These include teeth whitening for enhanced aesthetic appeal, restorative dentistry to repair and revitalize damaged teeth, and routine dental check-ups to ensure optimal oral health. Her specialty areas extend to crowns and bridges, which are crucial for restoring function and appearance to patients with missing or damaged teeth. She is experienced in tooth extraction, scaling and polishing to maintain gum health, as well as the application of veneers for aesthetic enhancements. Furthermore, she provides care for removable partial dentures and full dentures, catering to patients with varying dental needs. Multilingual in English, Hindi, Malayalam, and Tamil, Dr. Anu Joseph is prepared to communicate effectively with a diverse range of patients, ensuring comfort and understanding during their visit. Her dedication to ongoing professional development and patient care positions her as a trusted provider in the field of orthodontics and dentistry.

Expertise:
  • Functional appliance therapy
  • Clear aligners
  • Restorative dentistry
  • Teeth whitening
